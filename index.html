Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the driving force behind Bangladesh 2.0. By encouraging startups, research, and technology-based solutions, the nation can solve real-world problems efficiently. Creative thinking and smart ideas will shape a competitive global future.

Sustainability

Sustainability ensures development without harming future generations. Bangladesh 2.0 focuses on eco-friendly policies, climate resilience, and responsible resource management. A green mindset today secures a safer tomorrow.

Infrastructure

Strong infrastructure is the backbone of economic growth. Modern roads, bridges, ports, and smart cities will connect people and industries faster. Efficient infrastructure boosts trade, productivity, and national progress.

Empowerment

Empowerment means giving people the skills, education, and opportunities to succeed. Bangladesh 2.0 aims to uplift youth, women, and rural communities. When citizens are empowered, the nation becomes stronger.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 dreams of smart cities, clean energy, advanced industries, and strong global partnerships. It is a future where economic prosperity, social justice, and environmental balance exist together in harmony.

Digital Transformation

Over the last two decades, Bangladesh has made significant strides in transforming its digital landscape. Policies supporting internet access, e-governance, and ICT education have helped lay the foundation for a more tech-savvy, digitally inclusive nation.

Renewable Energy

In 1980, only 0.016% of Bangladeshis had electricity. Fast-forward 40 years and the figure has rocketed up to 92%. Only a few years ago, Bangladesh sat with countries like Mongolia in finding themselves with electrification levels below 90%.Bangladesh’s renewable energy policy journey began in 2008.
